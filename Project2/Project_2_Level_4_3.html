sky.

The

  free

          bird thinks of another breeze and the trade winds soft through the sighing trees and the fat worms waiting on a dawn bright lawn and he names the sky his own

caged

The

  caged

          bird sings with a fearful trill of things unknown but longed for still and his tune is heard on the distant hill for the caged bird sings of
